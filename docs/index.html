Little Legends
Little Legends in Teamfight Tactics are charming avatars that represent players in matches. They move around, collect carousel items, and use emotes to add flair. Players can unlock them via eggs or buy them, evolving their appearance over time. While purely cosmetic, they offer a fun way to personalize the game.
